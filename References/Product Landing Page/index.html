Locally Sourced

Our restaurant uses ingredients such as meat and vegetables sourced from local farmers and producers. We support local businesses, and so should you!

Our waitstaff and employees are paid healthy wages above the industry standard. We do this to take the burden off of our customers feeling the need to tip. We believe it is the employer's responsibility to provide a living wage.
